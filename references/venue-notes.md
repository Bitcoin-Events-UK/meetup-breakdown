# Venue Notes

Verified Bitcoin-accepting venues and key details for the newsletter.

## London

- **Hop Poles, W6** — 17-19 King St, London W6 9HR. Next to Hammersmith Tube. Big pub with terrace. Bitcoin accepted.
- **Pintus, SW11** — SW11 6QZ. Battersea Bitcoiners venue. Bitcoin accepted.

## Sheffield

- **Yellow Arch Studios** — 30-36 Burton Rd, Neepsend, Sheffield S3 8BX. Tea/coffee bitcoin accepted. Then moves to The Depo Bakery (fiat only).

## Bournemouth

- **Chaplins Cellar Bar** — 529 Christchurch Road BH1 4AG. Bitcoin accepted. Cogg Room.

## Birmingham

- **The Wellington** — Bennetts Hill, Birmingham B2 5SN. Brum Bitcoin & Beer venue.

## Nottingham / West Bridgford

- **O'Connors Irish Bar** — 33 Central Avenue, West Bridgford, Nottingham NG2 5GQ. ✅ Bitcoin accepted.
