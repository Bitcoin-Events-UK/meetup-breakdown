### **🕵️ The Satoshi Cipher -- Week 6**

**Prize:** 1,750 sats ⚡ 

**Cipher:** Morse Code uses dots (·) and dashes (--); letters are separated by spaces and words by a slash. Once you memorize common patterns like ·-- (A) and --·· (D), decoding is fast.

**History:** Co-invented by Samuel Morse in the 1830s, it sped up global communication via telegraph lines long before telephones existed.

`Puzzle: .-. ..- -. -. .. -. --. / -. -- -.. . ... `

Reply to claim the sats. 

**Bonus:** Solve in 10 minutes for double sats. 

Last Week's Winner: **Nick Morris** - solved "MINED ENERGY".
